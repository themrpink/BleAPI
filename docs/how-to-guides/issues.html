Known issues

CosmedBleDevice is null

When a CosmedBleDevice cannot be instatiated, and a null ojbect is repeatedly obtained instead, then stopping and restarting advertising on the remote device may solve the issue.

Error HRESULT: 0x80650003 from Service Changed characteristic operations

Although subscribable to indications, in the tests the following attribute produces an error when trying to subscribe to it: 2a05 (00002a05-0000-1000-8000-00805f9b34fb) (Service Changed characteristic) error read: Impossibile scrivere l’attributo. (Eccezione da HRESULT: 0x80650003) the error is given: when trying to subscrite to indications or unsubscribe(whish is flagged as possible) this is the uwp method causing the exception: WriteClientCharacteristicConfigurationDescriptorAsync(GattClientCharacteristicConfigurationDescriptorValue.Indicate)

Windows pairing

pairing: sometimes windows pairing window will appear asking to confirm a pin, but will usually fail pairing. If pairing fails without prompting then removing the remote device from the cached bluetooth devices may solve the issue.
